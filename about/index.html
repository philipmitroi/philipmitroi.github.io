About Me

I am a 21 year old creative who currently resides in Toronto. I’ve been involved in arts since I was a small child, throughout the years learning many useful skills that would now be classified under a digital communications category. To develop my skills in a professional setting I have floated around many post-secondary institutions, spending time at Ryerson University learning computer science, Humber College’s Digital Communications program, a bachelor’s degree that attemps to give a rounded education in the digital media and communications industry, learning skills such as video editing and logo design. I am currently studying Audio Engineering, at the Trebas Institute in Toronto, a lifelong passion of mine that I intend to develop aggressively in the next few years.
