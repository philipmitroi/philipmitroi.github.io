Some Updates

You probably noticed by now that my images don’t scale. This is the next step in the devellopment of this website, I am not the world’s greatest programmer, nor do I have the most experience deploying to the web, although design is not an issue. Throughout the programming of this site, I’ve learned a lot about Hugo, the generator I am using to create this website. Currently there is no native way to implmenet dynamic scaling to images, but the internet hasn’t slept on this.

In the next week, I plan to implement one of these two gallery solutions to the site for a better viewing experience. A gallery will allow me to consolidate more work in one page, and strengthen the intention of a personal website as a hub for my work, rather than stretching content across different platforms and sites.
