Selection from a retail catalog

A collection of images that were used for advertising and promo for my friend’s retail business.

Naturally, one of the first people I shot with was Stas. You can see other projects I’ve done with him on the website’s homepage, just look for his image! In a more corporate setting, you would probably find a team of people working on this project. Beyond the photographer, there would probably be a stylist and a team for hair/makeup, as well as someone to act as the project’s creative director. Luckily for us, Stas already has an affinity for luxury fashion and portraying it in a way that would make it appealing. As a person who is also interested in some of the brands that the store offers (Rick Owens, Supreme, Bape, etc), doing this project was a blast.
