Stas

A session with my friend Stas

Stas is one of my closest friends, he’s an emerging artist from Toronto who you can find on soundcloud under the moniker Yani. I think he and I go for a shoot at least once every week. On top of taking pictures for him, I also produce a great deal of music for him. In these industries I find it very important to have someone around who you can work with on anything, messages may be lost in translation should the aesthetic or message be limited to a single format. By allowing me to work with him on both visual and acoustic platforms, we achieve a unique aesthetic that is consistent all across the board of his brand, with more control than assembling content from sources produced by different people.
